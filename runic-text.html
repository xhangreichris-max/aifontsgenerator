What Is a Runic Text Generator?

Runic text uses Elder Futhark runes — the oldest form of the runic alphabets used by Germanic peoples from around the 2nd to 8th centuries. The Unicode standard includes the full Elder Futhark runic alphabet in its Runic block. Each Latin letter maps to a visually similar rune character. The result looks like ancient inscriptions while remaining readable to anyone familiar with the original Latin letters.
